SUBMISSION GUIDELINES


Please send us work that cares about the form it takes; work that fits oddly; work that might be unprofitable; work that might not find a home elsewhere.

Some writers we like: George Oppen, Karen Russell, Harryette Mullen, Sawako Nakayasu, Emily Dickinson, Dianne Seuss, Danez Smith, Saretta Morgan, Julio Cortazar, JJJerome Ellis, Rosmarie Waldrop, Maggie Nelson, Rebecca Curtis, Fred Moten, Isabella Hammad, Gustaf Sobin, Chen Chen, Matthew Zapruder, Earl Sweatshirt, Lorine Niedecker, Nikolai Gogol, Natalie Diaz, Etel Adnan.


For prose pieces, please send a maximum of 4,000 words per submission.
For poetry, send a maximum of 6 pages per submission. Each poem should begin on a new page.

Please submit work by emailing thebridge716@gmail.com. The subject-line should include your name, genre (poetry, prose, hybrid), and the word "SUBMISSION" somewhere in it.

Attach your work to the email as a .docx file. Please include a brief cover letter and bio in the body of the email.



Our response time is 1-2 months, but we'll try to respond even sooner in general. If your submission hasn't received a response after two months, give us a poke via email!
